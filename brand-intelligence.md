# Westinghouse Brand Intelligence

## Record

- **Official logo source:** Westinghouse corporate website asset: `https://westinghousenuclear.com/assets/img/westinghouse-logo-both-278x67.png`
- **Asset provenance:** Official Westinghouse corporate website, observed July 11, 2026.
- **Palette:** The official digital presence consistently uses deep navy, saturated corporate blue, bright cyan, white, and light neutral gray. Campaign values are employer-adjacent approximations (`#001e44`, `#005eb8`, `#00a3e0`) rather than a claim that these are a complete official brand-standard specification.
- **Typography character:** Modern geometric/humanist sans serif, strong weight contrast, compact navigation, large high-confidence headlines, and restrained technical copy.
- **Photography/illustration character:** Nuclear facilities, engineered systems, technical personnel, safety equipment, component detail, and human ingenuity; clean lighting with a technical-industrial tone.
- **Geometry and interface motifs:** Circular Westinghouse mark, disciplined grids, clean horizontal rules, broad white space, blue technical lines, and product/facility imagery.
- **Overall vibe:** Technical, established, safety-conscious, globally scaled, optimistic, and innovation-led without being playful.
- **Supportable assets:** The official logo is documented as a research and provenance source. It is intentionally not used as the campaign header or candidate identity mark to avoid implying endorsement or making the site resemble an official Westinghouse publication.
- **Original visuals:** The contour-based Executive Operating Envelope is original campaign artwork derived from the role's risk/constraint model and the company's blue technical visual grammar.
- **Recommended use by artifact:** Candidate-owned `RD` identity remains primary. Westinghouse cues appear through restrained palette, technical geometry, density, rules, and language. Resume and cover-letter branding remains deliberately subtle.

## Usage constraints

The campaign is an independent candidate work product. It must not imply Westinghouse authorship, sponsorship, approval, or endorsement. Official brand assets should not be altered, stretched, or used as the candidate's own mark. External assets must not be hotlinked in the production campaign.
