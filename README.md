# Westinghouse OPX Chief of Staff Candidate Campaign

Candidate campaign for Russell Dudek's application to the Westinghouse OPX Chief of Staff role in Cranberry Township, Pennsylvania.

## Campaign metadata

- **Suggested chat name:** Westinghouse – OPX Chief of Staff
- **Company:** Westinghouse Electric Company
- **Canonical role title:** OPX Chief of Staff
- **Campaign repository:** https://github.com/russelldudek/Westinghouse
- **Original job posting:** https://careers.westinghousenuclear.com/job/Cranberry-Township-OPX-Chief-of-Staff-NC/1405207900/
- **Print standard:** US Letter
- **Campaign thesis:** **Make execution legible before it becomes urgent.**
- **Operating model:** Executive Operating Envelope

The campaign proposes an **Executive Operating Envelope**: a role-specific operating model connecting commitments, constraints, decision rights, and learning so executive attention can focus on emerging systemic risk without centralizing every decision or adding unnecessary bureaucracy.

## Evidence integrity

Career facts are constrained to verified RoleForge candidate evidence. The campaign does not claim direct nuclear-industry experience. Because the full job-description text was not reliably available through public indexing during research, internal-remit assumptions are explicitly labeled as hypotheses for discovery.

## Brand posture

The campaign uses employer-adjacent visual cues from the official Westinghouse digital presence but retains a candidate-owned identity. The official logo is documented for provenance and intentionally not used as the campaign header to avoid suggesting sponsorship or impersonating an official Westinghouse publication.

## Publication

Designed as a static GitHub Pages campaign served from the repository root. GitHub Pages status must be verified independently; a successful commit does not prove deployment.
