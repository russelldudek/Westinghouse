# Westinghouse OPX Chief of Staff Candidate Campaign

Independent candidate campaign for Russell Dudek's application to the Westinghouse Electric Company OPX Chief of Staff role in Cranberry Township, Pennsylvania.

**Suggested chat name:** `Westinghouse - OPX Chief of Staff`  
**Canonical job posting:** `https://careers.westinghousenuclear.com/job/Cranberry-Township-OPX-Chief-of-Staff-NC/1405207900/`  
**Publication branch:** `main`  
**Print standard:** US Letter

## Campaign thesis

**Make execution legible before it becomes urgent.**

The campaign proposes an **Executive Operating Envelope**: commitments, constraints, decision rights, and learning made visible enough that executive attention can distinguish routine variance from systemic risk without centralizing every decision or weakening nuclear authority.

## Artifact set

- `index.html` - candidate vision, signature kinetic envelope, scenario states, simulator, stakeholder topology, scorecard, proof trail, objection, and executive questions.
- `resume.html` - exactly two-page role-aligned resume in print/PDF.
- `cover-letter.html` - exactly one-page cover letter in print/PDF.
- `interview-brief.html` - four-page interview thesis brief in print/PDF.
- `120-day-plan.html` - three-page entry plan in print/PDF.
- `styles.css`, `brand-tokens.css`, `app.js` - full-page visual system, brand tokens, interaction, responsiveness, accessibility, and reduced-motion behavior.
- `brand-intelligence.md`, `assets/brand/`, `assets/company/` - official-asset provenance, color and typography evidence, local logos, and official imagery.
- `docs/` - generated PDFs for every printable artifact.
- `artifact-manifest.json`, `campaign-metadata.json` - auditable route/file inventory and campaign metadata.

## Brand relationship

The official Westinghouse wordmark and imagery identify the target employer. The campaign is clearly labeled as independent candidate work by Russell Dudek and does not imply Westinghouse sponsorship, approval, authorship, employment, or endorsement. Official assets remain unmodified and locally committed.

## Evidence integrity

Career facts are constrained to `russelldudek/roleforge` candidate evidence. The campaign does not claim direct nuclear-industry experience. Because the complete internal job description was not reliably available through public indexing, company-specific operating recommendations are labeled hypotheses for discovery.

## Publication and audit

The static campaign is designed for GitHub Pages from `main` and `/ (root)`. Final audit records are committed alongside the campaign after rendered, PDF, main-branch, canonical-alignment, brand-fidelity, and deployment verification.
