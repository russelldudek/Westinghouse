INTERVIEW THESIS BRIEF

Make execution legible before it becomes urgent.

A discussion document for testing one idea: the OPX Chief of Staff can help Westinghouse scale by keeping commitments, constraints, decisions, and learning inside one executive operating envelope.

Mandate hypothesis

Nominal role

OPX Chief of Staff, Cranberry Township.

Actual mandate hypothesis

Turn a broad, safety-critical operating business into a more coherent executive system without adding unnecessary bureaucracy.

Evidence boundary: The public posting confirms the title and location, but the complete job description was not reliably retrievable through public indexing. Internal-remit recommendations are therefore hypotheses for discovery.

Westinghouse announced a DOE financing partnership tied to long-lead items for up to 10 AP1000 units.

Hard objection

“You have not spent your career inside a nuclear OEM or utility.”

Correct. The case is not false domain equivalence. It is adjacent high-consequence evidence: operating discipline, quality systems, regulated execution, cross-functional visibility, and mechanisms that make expert signals easier to hear without blurring nuclear authority.

Transfer principle: bring mechanism discipline, not borrowed authority. Learn nuclear context from the accountable experts; improve how their signals, dependencies, and decisions move through the executive system.
