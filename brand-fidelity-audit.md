# Brand Fidelity Audit

## Brand relationship

Westinghouse's official identity establishes who the campaign is for. Russell Dudek's Executive Operating Envelope establishes the candidate point of view. Every surface states that the work is an independent candidate campaign and does not imply Westinghouse authorship, sponsorship, approval, employment, or endorsement.

## Evidence and implementation

- Official blue and white Westinghouse 140-year wordmarks are stored locally under `assets/brand/` and remain unmodified.
- The blue wordmark is visible above the fold with `Candidate vision for OPX Chief of Staff` and `by Russell Dudek - independent candidate work`.
- Official AP1000 and laboratory images are stored locally under `assets/company/`, visibly used with accurate alt text, and never represented as Russell's work.
- `brand-tokens.css` uses source-sampled public Westinghouse CSS values for the company-recognition layer: `#253470`, `#12326A`, `#0675BC`, `#1EC2F3`, `#487C09`, `#FDD922`, and `#78272A`.
- Candidate-original light/surface colors are documented and subordinate.
- Westinghouse public CSS declares Gotham/GothamMedium. Proprietary font files were not copied. Aptos/Segoe UI/Helvetica Neue/Arial are documented structural substitutes, with brand behavior reproduced through hierarchy, case, tracking, weight, density, and rhythm.
- Logos are not animated, recolored, traced, filtered, shadowed, cropped, or combined with a RoleForge/Russell mark.
- Website, resume, cover letter, interview brief, 120-day plan, and PDFs share the same wordmark, tokens, typography decision, rules, and page furniture without becoming fake corporate letterhead.

## Rendered review

Source renders were inspected at 1440 x 900, 1280 x 800, 768 x 1024, 390 x 844, and reduced-motion mode. Company identity remains recognizable above the fold, independent-candidate status remains immediate, the role metaphor remains legible, and no horizontal overflow remains. The official identity is visually secondary to the thesis but cannot be mistaken for a generic RoleForge template.

Final live brand-fidelity status is recorded after deployment verification.
